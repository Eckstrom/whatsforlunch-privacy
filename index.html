Privacy Policy

What's For Lunch  ·  Last updated: March 2026

What's For Lunch is a free Android application designed to help you discover recipes based on the ingredients you already have at home. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

What Data Is Collected

What's For Lunch does not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or location. Your pantry items, personal recipes, shopping list, and app preferences are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

The app uses Google Firebase to host and serve recipe images. As a result, Firebase may collect anonymous technical data including device identifiers, app interactions, and usage statistics. This data is collected and processed by Google and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

What's For Lunch uses the following third-party service:

Google Firebase — used for hosting and delivering recipe images. Firebase may collect anonymous usage and diagnostic data as part of its standard operation. For more information on how Google handles this data, please review Google's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

No Ads

The app contains no advertisements of any kind. No advertising identifiers are accessed or used by the app itself.

Device Storage

All user-generated data — including pantry items, personal recipes, your shopping list, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Android's standard local database and shared preferences APIs. This data is only accessible to the app and is removed when you uninstall the application.

Children's Privacy

What's For Lunch does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The only data that may be collected is anonymous usage data via Google Firebase as described above.

Changes to This Policy

If the app is updated in a way that changes how data is handled, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. The date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised.
