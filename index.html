Privacy Policy — What's For Lunch

Last updated: May 4, 2026

What's For Lunch ("the app") is developed and maintained by Philip Eckstrom, of Techstrom Services. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including which screens are visited and general app usage patterns. This data contains no personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve the app experience.

Data You Enter

The following data is entered by you and stored locally on your device only. It is never transmitted to any server or shared with any third party:

  • Pantry items
  • Shopping list entries
  • Personal recipes you create
  • Dietary preferences and filters (such as Vegan, Gluten-Free, or other diet selections). These preferences are stored on your device only and are never collected, transmitted, or shared.

Feedback and Survey

If you choose to contact us via the in-app feedback option or complete the optional user survey, you may voluntarily provide your email address. This is used only to follow up on your feedback and is never shared with third parties.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, location, or any other personal identifiers
  • We do not collect or transmit health or fitness data
  • We do not sell or share any data with advertisers or third parties
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

Data Retention & Deletion

Locally Stored Data

All data you enter in the app — including pantry items, shopping list entries, personal recipes, and dietary preferences — is stored locally on your device and retained until you remove it. You can delete this data in the following ways:

  • In-app deletion: Delete individual pantry items, shopping list entries, or personal recipes directly within the app at any time.
  • Uninstall the app: Uninstalling What's For Lunch permanently removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • Clear app data: Go to Android Settings → Apps → What's For Lunch → Storage → Clear Data to erase all local app data without uninstalling.

Analytics Data

Anonymous analytics data collected via Google Firebase Analytics is retained according to Google's standard data retention policy (up to 14 months). Because this data is fully anonymous and not linked to your identity, it cannot be individually identified or deleted. You may limit analytics collection by opting out of ad personalization in your device's Google account settings.

Survey Email (Optional)

If you voluntarily provided your email address via the feedback survey, it is retained only for follow-up purposes. To request deletion, email us at philip@techstromservices.com with the subject "Delete My Data" and we will remove it within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics
  • Google Firebase Storage — used to serve recipe images provided by the developer. No user data is uploaded to or stored in Firebase Storage.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
