Privacy Policy

What's For Lunch  ·  Last updated: March 2026

What's For Lunch is a free Android application designed to help you discover recipes based on the ingredients you already have at home. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

No Data Collection

What's For Lunch does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no internet connectivity, no user accounts, and no backend servers. Everything you enter — your pantry items, saved recipes, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage and never leaves it.

No Third-Party Services

What's For Lunch does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. No SDKs from third parties with data collection capabilities are included in the app.

No Ads

The app contains no advertisements of any kind. No advertising identifiers are accessed or used.

Device Storage

The app stores data locally on your device (pantry items, personal recipes, shopping list, and settings) using Android's standard local database and shared preferences APIs. This data is only accessible to the app and is removed when you uninstall the application.

Children's Privacy

What's For Lunch does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If the app is updated in a way that changes how data is handled, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. The date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised.
