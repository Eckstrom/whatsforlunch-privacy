Privacy Policy

What's For Lunch  ·  Last updated: April 26, 2026

What's For Lunch is a free Android application designed to help you discover recipes based on the ingredients you already have at home. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Health and Food-Related Data

What's For Lunch accesses, stores, and processes food and nutrition-related data on your device. This includes recipe ingredients, pantry inventory items, shopping list items, ingredient quantities and unit measurements, serving sizes, and related food preparation information. Under Google Play policies, food-related data is classified as Health Data.

All health and food-related data you enter — including your pantry items, shopping list items, personal recipe notes, star ratings, serving size preferences, cooking timer states, and recipe progress — is stored exclusively on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers or shared with any third party. We do not collect, upload, share, or sell your food-related data.

The app uses this data solely to provide its core functionality: matching recipes to your available ingredients, managing your pantry and shopping list, tracking cooking progress, and personalizing your experience. This data is only accessible to the app and is removed when you clear the app's data or uninstall the application.

What Data Is Collected

What's For Lunch does not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, account credentials, location, contacts, or photos. No user account or sign-in is required to use the app.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Pantry inventory (ingredient names, quantities, and units)
  • Shopping list items (ingredient names, quantities, and units)
  • Personal recipe notes and star ratings
  • Favorite and bookmarked recipes
  • Cooking timer preferences and active timer states
  • App settings and preferences (temperature units, timer sounds, serving sizes, display options)

Automatically Collected Information

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. This may include:

  • Device type, operating system version, and screen resolution
  • App version and session duration
  • General usage patterns (screens viewed, feature engagement)
  • Crash and performance diagnostics
  • Approximate location (country or region level, derived from IP address — no precise GPS location is accessed)

This data is collected in aggregate, is anonymous, and cannot be used to identify you personally. It helps us understand how the app is used so we can improve the experience.

Third-Party Services

What's For Lunch uses the following third-party service:

Google Firebase — used for hosting and delivering recipe images (Firebase Storage) and collecting anonymous usage analytics (Firebase Analytics). Firebase may collect anonymous usage and diagnostic data as part of its standard operation. No user-entered food or health-related data is transmitted to Firebase. For more information on how Google handles this data, please review:

  • Google's Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Information

No Ads

The app contains no advertisements of any kind. No advertising identifiers are accessed or used by the app itself.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any of your personal or food-related data with third parties. The only third-party service integrated into the app is Google Firebase, which receives anonymous analytics data as described above. Your food and health-related data remains on your device at all times.

Data Retention and Deletion

All user-generated data — including pantry items, shopping list, personal recipes, notes, ratings, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Android's standard local database. This data is retained until you delete it within the app, clear the app's data through your device settings, or uninstall the application. Anonymous analytics data is retained by Google in accordance with their standard data retention policies.

Data Security

Because your food-related and personal data is stored entirely on your device, it is protected by your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We do not have access to this data. Network communications with Firebase (for analytics and image downloads) use industry-standard TLS encryption.

Children's Privacy

What's For Lunch is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The only data that may be collected is anonymous usage data via Google Firebase as described above.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Opt out of analytics: You can limit analytics collection through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Delete your data: All locally stored data can be deleted by clearing the app's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.
  • No account required: The app does not require registration or sign-in, so there is no account data to manage.

Changes to This Policy

If the app is updated in a way that changes how data is handled, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. The date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised.
